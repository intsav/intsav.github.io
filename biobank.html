Code Repositories

In the wake of open science, our priority has been the full publication of details of all methods developed in research so that they can be reproduced, criticised, and improved upon. We make all our research materials, including the datasets, code of experiments, and analyses, freely available at our GitHub page: github.com/intsav

Work with us

    Why work with us:
    • We are a team of deeply passionate, creative problems solvers. From AI researchers and data scientists, to engineers and clinicians throughout the country, we work with our partners to transform the AI technologies.
    • As part of our team, you can contribute to shaping how our world – through next generation advancements in AI.


    Entry requirements for PhD positions:
    • MSc degree with Merit or above or have equivalent postgraduate or research experience.
    • Applicants need to have a strong academic record. They should have a good ability in analytical methods and programming.
    • International applicants only: an IELTS score of 6.5 or higher (with no element under 6.0). Applicants with a previous degree obtained in the UK are exempt from this requirement.


    Available Postdoctoral and Research Assistant Positions:

    Scholarships
    VC Scholarships are available for PhD research positions for Home/UK students only. PhD programmes are funded for a 3-year period, and can start in Feb, May, and September each year. Funding is generous, including PhD bursaries at the standard rate and paid registration fees.
