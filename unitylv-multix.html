UnityLV-MultiX Dataset

About the Dataset

The images in this dataset were sourced from the Imperial College Healthcare NHS Trust and comprise 100 apical four-chamber (A4C) echocardiography videos collected in 2019. This dataset is gender-balanced (47% male, 53% female) with a median age of 60 years (IQR: 48.5â€“73.0). Each video has one end-diastolic (ED) and one end-systolic (ES) frame extracted, totaling 200 images. These were independently annotated by 11 experts for use as an independent external test dataset.

The dataset is publicly released under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 license to promote research and encourage the validation of deep learning models using multi-expert labeled data. Ethical approval was obtained from the Health Regulatory Agency.

The left image illustrates multi-expert annotations, while the right image shows the consensus curve.

Contents of the Dataset

  • Images folder: Contains 200 images (100 ED & 100 ES frames).
  • user-labels-all.csv: Expert annotations of 100 studies.
  • UnityLV_multiX_annotations_point_order_corrected.json: Reordered annotation coordinates.
  • UnityLV_multiX_consensus_curves.json: Consensus (average) label coordinates.
  • Rankings.json: Image rankings based on expert agreement.
