Project Overview

Project Goals

Join us in innovative research and development projects tailored for Undergraduate, MSc, Internship, and Computer Vision enthusiasts. Contribute to cutting-edge technologies and gain valuable experience in a collaborative environment.

Active Learning & Quality Ranking

Our projects may utilize active learning techniques to prioritize data annotation, and quality ranking systems to ensure high standards. Learn more about these methods: Active Learning and Quality Ranking.

Unity Labelling Platform

Some projects may leverage our streamlined labelling platform, accessible across devices. Experience seamless annotation and data processing: unityimaging.net.

Who Can Participate?

  • Undergraduate & MSc Students: Apply your academic knowledge to real-world projects.
  • Interns: Gain hands-on experience and professional development.
  • Computer Vision Enthusiasts: Explore and contribute to advanced computer vision techniques.

Your Role

  • Engage in research and development tasks specific to your project.
  • Contribute to data annotation, analysis, and algorithm development.
  • Collaborate with experienced researchers and developers.
  • Provide feedback and suggestions for project improvement.
