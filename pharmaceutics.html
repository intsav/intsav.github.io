Application of artificial intelligence in biopharmaceutical formulation

The aim of this cross-disciplinary project is to develop, characterise and formulate new antibody mimetics with the help of machine learning.

Applications are invited for a PhD position in the Biomedical Science and Computer Science at University of west London (UWL) to work as part of a multidisciplinary team, led by Dr Hanieh Khalili (h.khalili@uel.ac.uk) to develop and formulate novel antibody-based medicines and Prof Massoud Zolgharni (massoud.zolgharni@uwl.ac.uk) to use AI in prediction of the most suitable and stable formulation method for the developed molecules.
