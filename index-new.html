About IntSaV

The intelligent sensing and vision (IntSaV) research group is a specialized team dedicated to advanced research in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Big Data analysis. Our laboratory's primary focus is the development of cutting-edge and disruptive technologies to address healthcare challenges.

  • We are driven by a mission to significantly improve people's lives through our work in computer science.
  • Our research lies at the intersection of machine learning, artificial intelligence, computer vision, signal processing, and healthcare applications.
  • We strive to create impactful solutions in healthcare and biomedicine.

We believe in a collaborative and interdisciplinary approach, working with partners and funders to translate our research into real-world applications that benefit society.

Research Themes

Our research group embraces a multidisciplinary approach, exploring the synergy between various fields to drive innovation in healthcare and biomedicine.

Computer Vision

Developing advanced computer vision algorithms for image and video analysis, object recognition, and scene understanding.

Explainable and Responsible AI

Building AI systems that are transparent, interpretable, and ethical, ensuring fairness, accountability, and trust.

Machine Learning & Deep Learning

Utilizing machine learning and deep learning techniques for data analysis, pattern recognition, and predictive modeling.

Biomedical Image & Signal Processing

Developing innovative methods for processing and analyzing biomedical images and signals, extracting meaningful information for diagnosis and treatment.

Multimodal & Multisensory VR

Creating immersive and interactive virtual reality experiences that integrate multiple sensory modalities for enhanced perception and learning.

Mathematical Modeling

Developing mathematical models and simulations to understand complex biological systems and processes.
