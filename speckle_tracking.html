Development of optimisation-based approaches for speckle tracking

Our team has developed an an optimised-based block matching algorithm to perform speckle tracking iteratively. The proposed technique was evaluated using healthy and ischaemic cases.


Dataset

We used a publicly available synthetic echocardiographic dataset with known ground-truth (exact solutions) from several major vendors. Synthetic ultrasound images from 7 major vendors have been provided: GE, Hitachi-Aloka, Esaote, Philips, Samsung, Siemens, and Toshiba.


Download the dataset

Methods

In this paper, a new displacement estimation method is introduced by formulating the tracking as an optimisation problem that jointly penalises intensity disparity and motion discontinuity and is, therefore, more robust to the signal decorrelation when compared with previous approaches. The speckle tracking algorithm combines the BM algorithm with a smoothness constraint for a neighbourhood of kernels.
