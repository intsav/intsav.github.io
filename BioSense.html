Installation

Please go to https://biosense-3c6aa.nw.r.appspot.com/ using any browser and follow the instructions.

It is designed as an installable webapp and works using Safari (for MacOS, iPhone or iPad) or Google Chrome (for Windows, Linux, or Android devices). You will need a Google account to sign in.
